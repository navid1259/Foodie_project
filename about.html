About Us

We are four students from BCIT who share a strong interest in food, local culture, and discovering great places to eat. Our blog is all about exploring restaurants in Downtown Vancouver and sharing honest reviews based on our real experiences.

From casual lunch spots to hidden gems and popular restaurants, we visit different places, try the food, check the service, and review the overall experience. Our goal is to help other students, locals, and visitors find places that are worth trying.

This blog is more than just food reviews. It is also our journey as students who enjoy spending time together, exploring the city, and learning more about Vancouver through its food scene.

Every review on this website is based on our own visit, our own opinions, and our own taste. We hope our blog gives you useful ideas for your next meal in Downtown Vancouver.
